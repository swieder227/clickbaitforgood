Turning clickbait into donations.

Clickbait is the practice of spreading sensational headlines for the sake of page views. It has invaded our feeds, our lives and our cultural spectrum.

We’ve decided to turn it into something good and use clickbait to promote charities, support causes and encourage donations.

Join ‘Clickbait for Good’ by sharing a headline below.

This guy’s monthly bill will shock you!

For $30 you can hardly get a gym membership in the city. You can however bring clean water to a person in need, for a month. Help Charity Water continue their mission.

She fell for Mr. Perfect. You won’t believe what happened next.

In most cases of human trafficking, women are being seducedand then betrayed by their perpetrators. Love 146 is a non-profitorganization fighting to change that.

Shocking final words that will haunt you.

‘They will never accepted me for who I am’. Most suicidecases among LGBTQ youth are caused by lack of supportand acceptance. The Trevor Project is providing mental healthresources and a supportive environment to prevent that.

Outrageous! You won’t believe the size of this guy’s boat.

Thousands of refugees are crossing dangerous waters inundersized boats to flee conflict areas they once calledtheir homes. More than a millionaire’s yacht, this is whatwe should care and talk about.

This guy turned his cat into a rug.

The wild tiger is one of the most gracious felines in the world. Unfortunately, they are relentlessly poached, parts of their bodiesbeing used for medicine, folk remedies, and expensive rugs. Help WWF put an end to this.
